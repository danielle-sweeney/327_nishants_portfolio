I AM DEAD

I am a multimedia artist defining
consciousness through studying art,
aesthetics, and design.

I represent and explore the art of the human condition, whether it is suffering, bliss, or both.

The purpose of my art is to insinuate an urge to look
inward and reassess the current ego of ones self.

When one is without ego, they can experience their true self.
